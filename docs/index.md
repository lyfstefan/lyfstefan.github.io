## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in condensed matter theory at the University of California, Riverside.

My research focuses on moiré materials, electron-phonon coupling, twisted bilayer systems, and interaction-driven quantum phases.

## Research Interests

- Moiré materials
- Electron-phonon coupling
- Twisted bilayer graphene and TMDs
- Topology and correlated phases
